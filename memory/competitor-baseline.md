# Competitor baseline

Use this file as the long-term notebook for stable competitor context.

## How to use it

- Keep enduring positioning notes here.
- Do not paste huge article extracts.
- Keep each point attributable where possible.
- Move week-by-week observations into `weekly-change-log.md` instead.

## Starter baseline

### Mastercard
- Baseline role: client brand.
- Watch for: enterprise payment orchestration, commercial flows, merchant services, data services, fraud, identity, partnerships and cross-border B2B payments.

### Visa
- Watch for: commercial solutions, network capabilities, issuer/acquirer partnerships, B2B flows and acceptance infrastructure.

### Stripe
- Watch for: enterprise platform narrative, developer infrastructure, embedded payments, global acceptance and platform monetisation.

### Adyen
- Watch for: unified commerce, acquiring, risk, data-led merchant services and enterprise commerce proof.
